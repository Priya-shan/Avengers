Soul Stone

The Soul Stone, shrouded in mystery, holds the power to manipulate the essence of life and death. It can grant its possessor control over souls, allowing them to extract, manipulate, or even trap souls within an ethereal realm. The Soul Stone's enigmatic abilities offer a deep connection to the spiritual realm and the very fabric of existence. Those who seek to master the cycle of life and death are drawn to the Soul Stone's unfathomable powers.
